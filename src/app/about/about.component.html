Our Vision

ZIDI is a Solar All-In-one system that has been designed, developed & assembled locally here in Kenya. The Solar kit relates to the invention of an entertainment Solar All-In-one system which combines many functions and advantages. The device combines many functions into a compact unit. These functions include; entertainment, wireless Bluetooth connectivity, high quality sound, lighting source and a power bank with USB charging facility, solar charging via solar Photovoltaic (PV) module; integrated Maximum Power Point Tracking (MPPT) charge control; high efficiency Lithium-Ion rechargeable battery; high quality sound playback via FM Radio, USB, TF/SD Card, AUX and BluetoothTM; power bank facility in charging via USB port; BluetoothTM connectivity including A2DP and AVRCP; & high efficiency LED lighting.
